SECTION 13 : FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Questions Answered

Who is eligible to participate?

NYFAF is open to MDs, DOs, NPs, PAs, and RNs with an active medical license. Some international equivalents are accepted following license verification.

Are models provided for hands-on?

Yes, NYFAF provides all pre-screened patient models for the hands-on clinical portion. You are also welcome to bring a specific model if requested in advance.

Is there a group discount?

We offer a 15% tuition reduction for practices enrolling 3 or more clinicians in the same cohort.
