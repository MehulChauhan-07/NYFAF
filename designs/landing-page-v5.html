Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my visa is denied?

We offer a 100% deposit refund or credit towards a future session if you provide proof of visa denial at least 14 days before the course start date.

Do I need to bring my own models?

No. We provide all live models for the hands-on portion of the training, though students are welcome to bring a model if they prefer (please notify us in advance).

How close is the hotel to the training center?

Our preferred hotel partners are within a 5-10 minute walking distance from our Midtown training facility. You won't need to worry about NYC traffic or taxis.

Is this course for beginners or advanced practitioners?

We cover both Basic and Advanced techniques. The small class size allows us to tailor the level of instruction to each individual practitioner's experience level.
