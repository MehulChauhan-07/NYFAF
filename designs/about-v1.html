About Our
Training Programs

New York Facial Aesthetics Mentorship (NYFAC) offers structured, hands-on injectable training programs designed for licensed medical professionals at different stages of their aesthetic journey.

Our Core Principles

We maintain the highest standards in aesthetic medical education through four foundational pillars designed for clinical excellence.

menu_book

Evidence-Based Education

Rigorous academic standards rooted in the latest clinical research and peer-reviewed data.

health_and_safety

Anatomy & Safety

A deep emphasis on detailed facial anatomy to ensure maximum precision and patient safety.

groups

Live Model Experience

Supervised clinical experience with live models for hands-on, real-world practical skill mastery.

Small Group Instruction

Focused learning environments with strictly limited participants for personalized mentorship.

Clinical Excellence

Our programs combine didactic education with hands-on clinical training, ensuring participants develop both the knowledge and practical confidence required for real-world aesthetic practice.

NYC Based Training

Training is conducted in New York City and is led by experienced aesthetic medical professionals who actively practice in elite clinical settings.

Ethical Growth

NYFAC courses are designed to support responsible skill development, ethical practice growth, and uncompromising patient-centered care.
