Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a US Medical License to attend? expand_more
International doctors are welcome to attend for educational and observation purposes. For hands-on participation, we assist with temporary clinical observer status or temporary licensure documentation where applicable. Contact us for specific country requirements.
Where exactly is the training located? expand_more
Our clinical facility is located in Midtown Manhattan, near Grand Central Terminal. It is a state-of-the-art medical center fully equipped for aesthetic procedures and didactic sessions.
What is the cancellation policy? expand_more
We offer a full refund for cancellations made 30 days prior to the course. For cancellations within 30 days, we offer a credit towards the next available cohort.
